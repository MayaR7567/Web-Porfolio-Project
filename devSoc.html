Developer's Society: the Helpful Society

    I joined Developer's Society because they seemed really cool. That have a very active membership. They host tons of helpful and fun events I went to two of their python workshops, an Among Us game night, and a mainfram event.

    I think this is the most helpful society related to NTU. I feel like I can ask any member anything and recieve a helpful, polite reply. They are also the society that had helped me learn academically instead of just socially.
