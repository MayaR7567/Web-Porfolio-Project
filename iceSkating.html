How to Ice Skate without the Skate Part

    I joined the Ice Skating society because I have always had an interest in skating. I didn't realize at first that ice skating meant tricks but covid had my back with that. Due to covid and lockdowns the Ice skating group has yet to meet to skate. Instead we do a bunch of random things like, a trivia night, breakfast chat, and game night.

    This group is a lot of fun and my favourite society to hang out with so far. I am looking forward to us gettting on the ice in Janurary hopefully. We will see if I can learn any tricks
