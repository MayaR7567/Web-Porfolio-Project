Women's Development Programme: Confidence and Imposter Syndrome

Date: 10 Nov

    The Imposter Syndrome was not I term I knew until this workshop. The workshop was terrifying to realize just how many of us women feel like imposters even when we have earned where we are. I heard so many women say they didn't feel like they earned there place or that they shouldn't be where they are today. They should be, we should be! Even making this website right now I feel like an imposter, while i am sure many of my male classmates feel the opposite. This development programe really made me think of all the hard work I put in to get where I am. It is not luck, it's me.

    I loved this worshop on Assertiveness. The workshop teaches you all about the differences aggressive, passive aggressive, and assertive. We constantly become passive to out own emotions because we are afraid of others actions.
